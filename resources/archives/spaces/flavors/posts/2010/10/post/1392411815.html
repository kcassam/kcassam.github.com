Application iPhone Smart Password

October 24 2010, 11:27 PM  by Karim Cassam Chena√Ø

Given the same master password and parameter, Smart Password will always give you the same result. That’s so you don’t have to store your generated passwords anywhere: just come back here with your master password and the parameter. Because the password generation is performed inside the app, the master and generated passwords are never transmitted on the network.

- No storage whatsoever
- A very different password for each account and website
- No way to guess the Master Password
- No need to remember, write down, manage hundreds of passwords
- No device dependant : the free companion webapp at http://www.weedo.fr/pass/ works the same on every machine, yours or not, online or offline (via local copy), USB drive, iPhone/iPod Touch, iPad !
- Test for free thanks to the webapp http://www.weedo.fr/pass/ !! Buy only if you find it useful.
